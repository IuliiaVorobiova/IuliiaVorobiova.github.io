Mask - Media Invisible layer

The media produces a lot of informatio which is difficult to control. Is like an invisible spiderâ€™s web around your face. The more you consume information the more you lose the acuity of the perception.




Mask is a university project for the subject Contemporary History of Design. The chosen topic has to be symbolized by embodying the meaning into the project and choosing right process as well as respecting the materials for the production of the mask.

Process

The chosen materials are metal and paper both are formable and resilient. Metal - growing structure of the mask; Paper - is getting stronger when you apply many layers as the ideas are becoming stronger when are supported by the crowd.

Concept

The mask is growing dynamically around human face, each element is connected without presided boundaries. The process of the grows is very individual and depends on the media consumption behavior of each person.
